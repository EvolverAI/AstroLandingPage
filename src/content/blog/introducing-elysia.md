## What Makes Elysia Different

Most AI assistants give generic responses. Elysia is different — it's trained on **your** documents, your FAQs, your product catalogue, and your brand voice. The result is an assistant that genuinely represents your company.

### Features at a Glance

- **Custom knowledge base**: Upload PDFs, Word docs, web pages, or YAML — Elysia learns from them all.
- **Multi-language**: Fluent in English, Italian, German, and more.
- **Embeddable anywhere**: Drop a single script tag into your website.
- **Escalation paths**: When Elysia can't help, it hands off gracefully to a human.
